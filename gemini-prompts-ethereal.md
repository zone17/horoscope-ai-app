# Gemini Logo Prompts — Ethereal Pastel Aesthetic
## For gettodayshoroscope.com social media profiles

Use in Google AI Studio (ai.google.dev) or Gemini app.
Settings: 1:1 square, highest resolution.

---

## Prompt 1: Crystal Zodiac Wheel (closest to your reference images)

```
Design a circular social media profile logo for an astrology brand. Light cream/white background with a soft ethereal glow. The design features a delicate zodiac wheel with 12 segments in soft watercolor pastels — lavender, mint green, baby blue, soft peach, pale rose, and sage. Each segment has a subtle color wash like wet watercolor on paper. At the center is a thin gold line-art starburst with a small crescent moon. Concentric thin gold circles radiate outward. The overall feel is airy, luminous, spiritual, and premium — like a luxury wellness brand. Soft diffused lighting, no harsh edges, everything blends gently. No text. Think Co-Star app meets glossy editorial astrology. Clean, minimal, ethereal. The outer edge has a subtle white border with soft shadow giving it a floating, 3D quality.
```

## Prompt 2: Celestial Crystal

```
Design a circular social media profile logo for a modern astrology brand. Soft white and lavender gradient background with an ethereal glow. At center is a luminous crystal or gemstone shape (diamond/rhombus form) with iridescent purple, pink, and blue refractions — like light passing through a prism. Behind the crystal are thin geometric lines forming a sacred geometry pattern (overlapping triangles or a star). Around the crystal, arrange subtle zodiac symbols in soft purple, spaced evenly in a circle. The entire design has a dreamy, glowing, spiritual quality — soft focus, pastel palette of lavender, lilac, baby pink, and pale blue. No text. Premium wellness aesthetic, delicate and airy. Thin white outer border. The crystal should appear to glow from within with soft light bloom.
```

## Prompt 3: Watercolor Cosmos

```
Design a circular social media profile logo for an astrology brand. Clean white background with a soft circular watercolor wash in the center — blending from pale peach/coral on one side through sage green in the middle to soft lavender/blue on the other side, like a gentle sunrise-to-moonrise gradient. At the center, a delicate gold line-art crescent moon nestled with a small star, drawn with thin elegant strokes. Around the edges, tiny gold dots arranged in constellation patterns. The watercolor is soft and organic with natural bleeding edges, like real paint on paper. No text. The aesthetic is modern, spiritual, feminine, premium — like a high-end wellness brand or editorial astrology magazine. Soft and luminous with natural light. Thin subtle border.
```

## Prompt 4: Glowing Zodiac Ring

```
Design a circular social media profile logo for a horoscope brand. Soft white/cream background with a gentle luminous glow. Features a circular ring of 12 zodiac symbols, each in a different soft pastel color — peach for Aries, sage for Taurus, sky blue for Gemini, lavender for Cancer, warm coral for Leo, mint for Virgo, pale blue for Libra, dusty purple for Scorpio, coral-orange for Sagittarius, teal for Capricorn, icy blue for Aquarius, lilac for Pisces. Each symbol sits inside a soft, frosted glass-like circle with a subtle watercolor glow behind it. At the center, thin gold concentric circles with a small gold starburst. The overall feel is ethereal, modern, airy, and premium. Like frosted glass with soft backlighting. No text. White outer border with gentle drop shadow.
```

## Refinement Prompts (send as follow-ups)

After generating, try these to dial it in:
- "Make it softer and more ethereal, like light shining through frosted glass"
- "Add more of that iridescent lavender and mint watercolor quality"
- "Make the gold lines thinner and more delicate"
- "Soften all edges, nothing should look sharp or digital"
- "More luminous glow from the center, like it's lit from within"
- "Make the pastels more muted and sophisticated, less saturated"
- "Add a subtle white vignette around the edges"
