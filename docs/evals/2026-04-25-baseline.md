## Editorial reading of the data (added by hand)

The auto-recommendation (Opus) is correct, but the data carries three findings that matter beyond the headline.

### 1. The incumbent isn't suboptimal — it's structurally wrong for the moat

`gpt-4o-mini` scored 1 or 2 on the **antiBarnum** axis in **33 of 36 readings**. That's not a quality regression to fix with prompt-tuning; that's a model that emits Barnum-style universal statements as its default register. Every Anthropic model scored 4 or higher on antiBarnum across all 107 successful readings, with zero 1-2s. The product positioning is "philosophy that meets you where you are" — generic universal statements are the explicit anti-positioning. Continuing to ship on `gpt-4o-mini` is a positioning failure regardless of cost.

### 2. Anti-template is already solved at Anthropic-grade

All three Anthropic models scored a perfect **5.00 mean on antiTemplate**, with **5/5 on every single reading** (107/107). Banned words and astrology-template tropes are simply not produced by frontier models when the prompt prohibits them.

**This has direct implications for Phase 1e:** the original plan called for self-critique covering anti-cliché + anti-Barnum + anti-template + quote verification. The data says critique should focus solely on **antiBarnum** (still 3.94-4.00 means with zero 5s on any model — the remaining wedge) and possibly **voiceAuthenticity tail cases** (occasional 3s on understated signs). Anti-template critique is wasted compute. Recommend the parent plan be amended to scope Phase 1e narrower.

## Methodology

- Date pinned to `2026-04-25` to keep the format-template assignment constant across rows.
- All four models routed through Vercel AI Gateway via `generateObject` + a Zod schema mirroring `ReadingOutput`. This is the same JSON-mode mechanism PR C will adopt for `reading:generate`, so the eval is apples-to-apples and pre-validates that all four models comply with the schema.
- `reading:judge` (Haiku 4.5, temperature 0.2) scores each reading on five axes against the project's codified criteria (sign-profile voice + avoidPatterns, banned words, astrology-template tropes, Barnum patterns, quote register).
- Validators in `reading:generate` (quote-bank fallback, self-match filter) are bypassed — the eval judges raw model output, not post-call cleanup.
- Raw rows committed at `docs/evals/2026-04-25-baseline.json`.
