## Tips for Best Results

1. Generate each prompt 3-4 times and pick the best version
2. If the first result isn't perfect, reply with refinements like:
   - "Make the gold more metallic and luminous"
   - "Increase the contrast between the background and the main element"
   - "Make it bolder and more eye-catching at small sizes"
   - "Clean up the edges to be perfectly crisp"
3. Once you have your favorite, ask Gemini to regenerate it at the highest resolution
4. For social media uploads, these sizes work best:
   - Instagram: 320x320px (but upload at least 1000x1000 for quality)
   - Facebook: 170x170px display (upload 1000x1000)
   - TikTok: 200x200px display (upload 1000x1000)
   - X/Twitter: 400x400px (upload 1000x1000)

All platforms accept the same square image — just upload the 1000x1000 version everywhere.
